Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Jobney app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Jobney (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a mobile application service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

To provide you with the best job application tracking experience, we collect and store only the information you explicitly provide about your job applications, tasks, and events. This data is stored securely and is only used to provide you with the Application's services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and is accessible only through your authenticated account.

Third Party Services

The Application uses Apple's App Store services for managing in-app purchases. Apple has its own Privacy Policy which you can review on their website.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the bottom of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support channels in the app.

This privacy policy is effective as of March 9, 2025.
