How Does It Work?

Step 1. Add New Applications

Start by adding your job applications to Jobney. Input company details, position, application date, and any relevant notes. Keep everything organized in one place.

Step 2. Track Application Status

Update the status of your applications as you progress. From 'Applied' to 'Interview Scheduled' to 'Offer Received', stay on top of every stage.

Step 3. Schedule Events & Tasks

Set up interview reminders, follow-up tasks, and application deadlines. Our calendar integration ensures you never miss an important date.

Step 4. Monitor Your Progress

View insights about your job search journey, track success rates, and identify which strategies are working best for you.

Step 5. Create Message Templates

Build your library of custom message templates. Create and personalize templates for follow-up emails, thank you notes, and other communications to match your style.
